## The rhythm that compounds

Two buttons carry the whole system:

- **Plan my day** (`/today`) — every morning. Your AIOS reads your notes, your projects, your carries, and writes a one-screen plan.
- **Close the day** (`/close-day`) — every evening. Captures what shipped and what you learned, so tomorrow starts smarter.

A day without `/today` is a day your AIOS doesn't know about. The banner at the top of the panel will nudge you at the right times.
