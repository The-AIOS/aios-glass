## Where Glass looks

Glass reads your AIOS framework live from **`~/aios`** (setting: `aiosGlass.frameworkPath`). Nothing is hardcoded — your agents, commands, and skills appear because the files exist.

**Connected?** The panel's counters tell you: if *Launch an agent* / *Load a skill* / *Run a command* show numbers, you're live.

**Zeros everywhere?** Your AIOS lives somewhere else — point the setting at it.
