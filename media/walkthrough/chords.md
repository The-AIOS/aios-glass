## One leader, twenty moves

Everything is behind one chord: **⌘⌥G** then a key (Ctrl+Alt+G on Windows/Linux).

The essentials: **Q** ask AIOS · **D** daily ritual · **F** frequent tasks & routines · **A** launch agent · **`*`** the palette (fuzzy-search *everything* launchable) · **H** show/hide Glass.

The full map lives in the panel's **⌨ Key shortcuts** section — and in the cheatsheet.
