## Your AIOS, clickable

The **AIOS Glass panel** is home: daily rituals, live sessions, quick tasks, your calendar, what Claude's learned — every card surfaces something your AIOS already knows how to do.

**Tip:** drag the panel to the **secondary side bar** (right edge) so it lives beside your editor and terminal instead of replacing the file explorer.

> Glass, not engine — the panel never reimplements your AIOS. It triggers it.
