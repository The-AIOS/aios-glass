## Your team of specialists

Your AIOS ships agents — lawyer, content-writer, market-researcher, deck-builder, and any you or your company add. **Launch an agent** wears its hat with a task; **Spawn a session** opens parallel workers.

Watch the **Running** card: every live session with a breathing status dot (working / ready / needs you), and hover actions to **interrupt**, **close-session** (capture its work to your daily note), or **kill**.
