## The magic entry

Type what you need — **Claude matches your ask to the right context & tools in your AIOS, and puts them to work.**

Try: *"prep tomorrow's investor call"* · *"post about our launch"* · *"i need a social media strategy"*

It's also the fallback in every picker: a search that matches nothing offers **Ask AIOS** instead of a dead end. Each ask runs in its own named session (`ask-social-media-strategy`), visible in the Running list.
